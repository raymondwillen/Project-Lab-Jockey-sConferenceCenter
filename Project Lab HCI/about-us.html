About Us

Discover the Essence of Jockey's Conference Hall!

Our History

Established in 2000 (8 January 2000), The history of Jockey's Conference Hall is steeped in tradition and a legacy of hosting remarkable events. Since its inception, our venue has been an integral part of the community, welcoming esteemed guests from various industries and sectors.Jockey's Conference Hall was originally a small gathering place known for its warm hospitality and impeccable service. Over the years, as the demand for a larger and more sophisticated venue grew, the hall underwent a series of expansions and renovations to meet the evolving needs of its clients. Throughout its history, Jockey's Conference Hall has played host to an array of prestigious conferences, seminars, exhibitions, and celebrations. Renowned business leaders, industry experts, and influential figures have graced our halls, sharing their knowledge and expertise within our walls.

Our Achievement

Jockey's Conference Hall takes pride in presenting the esteemed Certificate of CHSE Standards. This distinguished recognition is awarded to organizations that have consistently demonstrated an unwavering commitment to providing exceptional conference and event services. At Jockey's Conference Hall, we consider this certificate as both an honor and a responsibility. It fuels our passion to continually evolve, innovate, and go above and beyond in creating extraordinary events that leave a lasting impression on our guests. As we move forward, we remain committed to upholding the high standards that this certificate represents. We are dedicated to providing an exceptional platform for knowledge-sharing, collaboration, and celebration. We invite you to join us on this remarkable journey and experience the unrivaled excellence that Jockey's Conference Hall has to offer.
